About Safe Zone Visualizer

Safe Zone Visualizer is a professional utility built by and for video editors. Navigating the constantly changing UI dimensions of platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts can be frustrating when you are trying to ensure your keyframes, text, and masks remain visible.

This tool was developed to streamline the workflow for creators using high-end software like DaVinci Resolve and Adobe Premiere Pro. Instead of guessing or relying on static PNG overlays that clutter your timeline, you can now drag and drop your draft exports directly into your browser to verify placement instantly.

100% Local Processing

Privacy and client confidentiality are critical in freelance video editing. That is why this tool processes everything client-side. Your videos never leave your device, and no files are ever uploaded to our servers.
